About Us

Turning tedious attendance into a fun QR code game where students check in faster than you can say “present!”

By leveraging QR code technology, our system saves time, enhances accuracy, and simplifies administrative tasks. Services

Add students, track attendance with a scan, and keep everything in order—no magic wands needed, just our awesome system!

Efficient Student Management

Teachers can easily add new students to the system, ensuring that their information is up-to-date from the first day of school. This feature simplifies student enrollment and keeps records organized.

Comprehensive Attendance Tracking

Our system allows teachers to view attendance data by major and section. It also displays the list of students present in each section, providing a clear overview of attendance records.

Seamless QR Code Integration

Once new students are added, the system generates a unique QR code for them. Students can scan this QR code on the teacher’s computer to log their attendance, which is automatically recorded according to their section and timetable.

Secure Data Storage

All attendance and student information is securely stored in our system. This ensures that data is protected and easily accessible for future reference, supporting efficient administration and record-keeping.

Our Challenges

QR Code Generation and Distribution

Ensuring each student receives a unique and scannable QR code. Preventing duplication or misuse of QR codes.

QR Code Scanning

Implementing reliable and quick scanning technology. Dealing with issues like poor lighting or damaged QR codes that may affect scanning.

Integration with Timetable

Ensuring the system accurately matches scanned data with the timetable. Handling dynamic schedules and any changes in the timetable.

Data Accuracy and Security

Ensuring that the attendance data recorded is accurate and free from errors. Protecting student data and attendance records from unauthorized access.

Frequently Asked Questions

We got a lot of questions from users and here're the answers

How many QR codes does each student receive?

Each student receives a unique QR code based on their student ID

Can students view attendance for every section and subject?

No, students can only view attendance for students in their respective section.

On which device will the QR codes be scanned?

QR codes will be scanned using a teacher's laptop.

Can the QR code attendance system be cheated?

No, students must scan their QR codes individually in front of the teacher, ensuring accurate attendance records.

Can data be lost in the system?

We have implemented robust data backup and recovery procedures to minimize the risk of data loss. Regular backups are performed, and data is stored securely to ensure its integrity and availability.
